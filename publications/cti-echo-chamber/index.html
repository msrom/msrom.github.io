The CTI Echo Chamber: Fragmentation, Overlap, and Vendor Specificity in Twenty Years of Cyber Threat Reporting

Manuel Suarez-Roman, Francesco Marchiori, Mauro Conti, Juan Tapiador

Summary

Large-scale analysis of more than 13,000 open CTI reports revealing fragmentation, overlap, and vendor-specific observational biases across two decades of threat reporting.

My contribution

Led dataset construction, LLM-assisted feature extraction, and analysis of actor–victim dynamics and vendor coverage effects.
