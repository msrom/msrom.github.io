Hesperus is Phosphorus: Mapping Threat Actor Naming Taxonomies at Scale

Gonzalo Roa, Manuel Suarez-Roman, Juan Tapiador

Summary

HiP integrates 17 vendor taxonomies and thousands of threat-actor aliases to study naming inconsistency and support normalization at scale.

My contribution

Co-designed the HiP methodology, built large-scale CTI datasets, and analyzed alias proliferation across vendors and time.
