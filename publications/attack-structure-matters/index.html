Computers & Security, 2025

Attack structure matters: Causality-preserving metrics for Provenance-based Intrusion Detection Systems

Manuel Suarez-Roman, Juan Tapiador

Summary

Introduces SDTED, a similarity metric for provenance graphs that preserves causal structure when evaluating intrusion detection and attack-graph reconstruction.

My contribution

Proposed SDTED, evaluated it on DARPA engagement scenarios, and open-sourced the implementation to improve reproducibility in PIDS research.
